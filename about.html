The Volunteering Committee of Vidyalankar Polytechnic is a dynamic student-driven body that plays a crucial role in organizing and managing college events, social initiatives, and community outreach programs. From cultural and technical fests to awareness campaigns and welfare activities, the committee fosters leadership, teamwork, and a spirit of service. Volunteers actively contribute to creating a vibrant and inclusive campus environment while gaining hands-on experience in event coordination, social responsibility, and professional networking.

Who   We   Are

We’re the people who believe in second chances. Who believe that we are better together. Who believe in people, until they believe in themselves again.

We are a passionate group of students committed to organizing and managing various events, social initiatives, and community-driven activities. Through teamwork and dedication, we create a vibrant and engaging campus environment while developing essential leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills. Our efforts extend beyond the college premises, as we actively participate in outreach programs, social welfare activities, and awareness campaigns to make a meaningful impact on society.

We take pride in being the backbone of college events, fostering collaboration, responsibility, and a spirit of service among students. From cultural fests and technical workshops to social welfare initiatives, we ensure smooth execution and meaningful participation. Volunteering allows us to gain hands-on experience in event management, public engagement, and teamwork while building a strong network within and beyond the institution.

We believe in creating a positive and inclusive environment where students can grow, contribute, and make a difference. Our commitment lies in organizing impactful events, supporting college activities, and engaging in community service. By working together, we enhance our problem-solving abilities, leadership qualities, and social responsibility, shaping ourselves into well-rounded individuals prepared for future challenges.

Vision &  Mission

We envision a dynamic and inclusive community where students actively contribute to society through meaningful engagement across eight key domains. Our goal is to foster leadership, teamwork, and social responsibility while creating a positive impact in diverse areas:

Vision

We aim to cultivate a culture of responsibility, leadership, and service, where students actively contribute to society through impactful initiatives. By engaging in diverse domains, we envision a future where learning goes beyond classrooms, fostering well-rounded individuals dedicated to making a difference.

Mission

Our mission is to empower students by providing opportunities to participate in social, cultural, and educational initiatives. Through teamwork and hands-on experiences, we strive to enhance skills, promote awareness, and drive positive change in both the campus and the wider community.

Values

We believe in teamwork, leadership, and compassion as the foundation of our efforts.
Integrity, inclusivity, and commitment drive us to create meaningful projects that uplift society while fostering personal and professional growth in every volunteer.
